CUTTING EDGE TECHNOLOGY
Polaris is powered by our ever evolving proprietary AI and Machine Learning engine to ensure cyber attacks are stopped before they can even begin. Instead of focusing on threats, let us help you focus on what really matters, your business.
EASY TO DEPLOY
Deploying Polaris is simple and only takes minutes. After a simple change to the domain’s name-servers or CNAME, the WAAP will begin protecting your website, offering you an overview of your website’s security stats on the dashboard as well as on the WAAP mobile application.
EASE TO USE
The intuitive WAAP dashboard makes information easy to access, interpret, and act upon, even for those newest to the cyber defense community. System features such as security rules are easy to customize with just a few clicks.

How Polaris Helps Your Business

Polaris is a roadblock in the cyber attack killchain’s first stage - conducting reconnaissance on potential targets of opportunity. As a cloud-based platform, we monitor trends across web applications to predict and prevent the attack before damage can be done. Highly customizable to your specific needs and available 24/7, Polaris is an essential part of any business’ security solutions in today’s digital age.
WEB APPLICATION SECURITY
Polaris protects your website from cyber attacks such as Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS), SQL Injection, Zero-Day attacks, and other OWASP Top 10 vulnerabilities before your application server is exploited. Polaris comes pre-configured with default expert-crafted protection rules, which means once enrolled, you’re defended immediately. If needed, you can also customize rules to easily fit your security policy requirements.
ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE
We use application specific machine and behavioral learning techniques such as “fingerprinting” to differentiate between legitimate and malicious web requests. Constantly evolving, our AI can identify and act upon cyber attacks targeting web applications that are woven into innocent looking traffic - traffic that slips right through traditional cyber security defenses.
THREAT INTELLIGENCE
Polaris utilizes a variety of trusted cyber intelligence sources to aggregate, compare, and analyze threat data to form a more comprehensive detection and defense strategy. Our threat intelligence capabilities grow alongside evolving enemy threats to stay up to date, forming a more comprehensive advance warning system.
