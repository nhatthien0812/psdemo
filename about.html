About Us

Polaris was formally founded in 2019 by a small team of passionate cybersecurity practitioners to develop and commercialize web security solutions. Based in both Vietnam and Singapore, the team developed the Polaris’ Web Application and API Protection (WAAP) to defend your web presence against the growing threat of cyber attackers.

Our Team
Tin Nguyen
CEO, Co-Founder
Tin has been serving the tech and cybersecurity communities as a co-founder and executive leader, most recently as the Vice President of Operations at Blackpanda, a Singapore based Digital Forensics and Incident Response firm. Prior to a career in the public sector, Tin was a Special Agent in the Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI) and an Infantry Officer in the United States Marine Corps. He completed undergraduate programs from both the Citadel and the SANS Technology Institute, and attended Georgetown University’s Cybersecurity Risk Management graduate program.
Thanh Nguyen
Chairman, Co-Founder
Thanh is CIO at VNG Corp and founder of VNSecurity. He is a well-recognized researcher with 20 years of security experience focused on large-scale application, firmware, chipset, and micro-architecture. He was a CPU Security Architect at Intel Corporation to secure several Intel technologies including Mobile & SoC platforms, vPro & Management Engine, Ivybridge and Haswell microarchitecture.
